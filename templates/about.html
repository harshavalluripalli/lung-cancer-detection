What is Lung Cancer?

Lung cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the lungs. It is one of the most common types of cancer and can be life-threatening. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and better outcomes.

Symptoms of Lung Cancer

  • Persistent cough that worsens over time
  • Chest pain that may worsen with deep breathing, coughing, or laughing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
